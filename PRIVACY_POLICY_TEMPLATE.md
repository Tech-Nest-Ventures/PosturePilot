# Privacy Policy for PosturePilot

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

## Introduction

PosturePilot ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our macOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Access
PosturePilot requires access to your device's camera to analyze your posture in real-time. 

**Important**: 
- All video processing happens **locally on your device**
- **No video or images are stored** on your device or transmitted anywhere
- **No video or images are sent to any server** or third-party service
- The camera feed is used **only for real-time posture analysis**

### Data Storage
PosturePilot stores the following data locally on your device:
- **Posture metrics** (measurements like head position, neck tilt, shoulder alignment)
- **Posture logs** (timestamps and status of your posture checks)
- **Calibration data** (your baseline posture measurements)

All data is stored in your app's local data directory and is never transmitted outside your device.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:
- Provide real-time posture monitoring and feedback
- Display posture metrics and trends
- Send local notifications when posture needs adjustment
- Maintain your posture history for personal reference

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties.** All processing and storage happens entirely on your device.

## Third-Party Services

PosturePilot uses the following third-party services:

### MediaPipe Pose Detection
- **Service**: Google's MediaPipe Pose library
- **Purpose**: Real-time pose detection and analysis
- **Data**: Video frames are processed locally using MediaPipe's on-device models
- **Privacy**: MediaPipe processes data locally; no data is sent to Google servers

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access your data**: All data is stored locally in your app's data directory
- **Delete your data**: You can delete the app at any time, which removes all stored data
- **Control camera access**: You can revoke camera permissions in macOS System Settings

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- No network transmission of personal data
- No cloud storage or external servers involved

## Children's Privacy

PosturePilot is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
